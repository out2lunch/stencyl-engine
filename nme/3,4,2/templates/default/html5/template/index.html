How to View the Console

Chrome: 'CTRL-SHIFT-J' or 'ALT-COMMAND-J' (Mac)

Firefox: 'CTRL-SHIFT-K' or 'ALT-COMMAND-K' (Mac)

Internet Explorer: 'F12, then CTRL-3'

Nothing appearing?

Refresh a couple times. If that fails, check the Console as directed above. If you're stuck, check out our HTML5 guide.
